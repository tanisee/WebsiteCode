From NY to Argentina

Argentina is 5,500 miles away from New York! A non-stop flight would take around 11 hours. You can get to Argentina by car, however it would be a very long drive and you would have to ship your car when reaching the Darien Gap

Top 5 Attractions in Argentina

  1. Iguazú Falls
    • Made up of more that 250 waterfalls
    • Much taller and twice the width of Niagra Falls
  2. Perito Moreno Glacier
    • A 30-kilometer-long ice formation
  3. The Towns of Recoleta, La Boca, and Tango
    • Filled with many museums for both adults and kids
    • Home of the most colorful neighborhood in Buenos Aires
  4. Tierra del Fuego National Park
    • Tons of trails with mixed difficulty levels, a hiker's paradise
  5. The Beaches of Mar del Plata
    • Beautiful beaches that strech for over 8 kilometers
    • Experience amazing wildlife, like sea lions, at these beaches
